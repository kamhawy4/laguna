# Product Requirements Document (PRD)


## 1. Overview

**Product Name:** LAGUNA LIFE – Real Estate Website
**Company Name:** LAGUNA LIFE
**Industry:** Real Estate – UAE
**Languages:** English (EN), Arabic (AR)
**Currencies:** AED, USD, EUR

**Product Scope:**
Design and develop a fully dynamic real estate website powered by a custom or headless CMS. All website pages, content, projects, pricing, media, and SEO data will be managed entirely through the CMS with no hardcoded content.

**Goal:** Build a scalable, bilingual, multi-currency real estate platform that allows LAGUNA LIFE to manage and update all website data independently through the CMS.

## 2. Objectives

* Showcase off-plan projects clearly and attractively
* Generate high-quality leads through forms and CTAs
* Educate users about areas, projects, and investment benefits
* Build credibility via team, testimonials, and blogs
* Support SEO and content marketing

## 3. Target Audience

* Local UAE buyers
* International investors
* First-time property buyers
* Real estate investors looking for off-plan opportunities

## 4. Key Success Metrics (KPIs)

* Lead form submissions
* Project inquiry conversions
* Time spent on project detail pages
* Blog engagement (views, shares)
* Bounce rate reduction

## 8. Functional Requirements

### 8.1 Website & CMS Architecture

* Website must be fully CMS-driven (no static/hardcoded content)
* CMS controls all content displayed on the website
* API-based integration between CMS and website (headless approach preferred)

### 8.2 CMS Capabilities

* Centralized CMS to control all website content and data across pages
* Manage pages (Home, Services, About Us, Team, Testimonials)
* Manage Projects / Developments (listing & details)
* Manage Area Guides
* Manage Blogs & categories
* Media management (images, videos, PDFs)
* SEO metadata per page and per language
* Language content management (EN / AR)
* Currency-based price management (AED / USD / EUR)
* Role-based access (Admin, Editor)
* Draft / publish workflow

### 8.4 Accounts & Access Management

* Proper user access and permissions for LAGUNA LIFE team

* Documentation handover for all accounts and credentials

* Multi-language support (EN / AR) with RTL handling for Arabic

* Multi-currency support (AED / USD / EUR) with configurable exchange rates

* Lead management (CRM integration ready)

* Responsive design (mobile-first)

* Fast loading & optimized media
